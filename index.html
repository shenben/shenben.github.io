Biography

My name is Xingguo PANG/,ʃɪŋ'gwəʊ'pɑŋ/, but you can also call me Steven. I am currently a 3rd-year Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at the University of Macau (UM), under the supervision of Professor Xiaobo Zhou.

Previously, I received my bachelor degree in Shandong University(SDU), and had the fortune to be advised by Prof. Weiguo Liu.

My recent research focus on Heterogeneous Memory Management for cloud-native applications and serverless computing.
